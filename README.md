🎯 Problem Statement
In today's always-on work culture, burnout has become an invisible epidemic. Most people don't realize they're overdoing it until it's too late. The gradual buildup of overwork goes unnoticed day by day — back-to-back meetings, skipped lunch breaks, late-night emails — and over time, this takes a serious toll on mental and physical health.
There is currently no proactive, passive system that monitors individual work patterns and warns people early enough to make a difference.

77% of professionals have experienced burnout at their current job
Burnout costs the global economy an estimated $322 billion annually in lost productivity
Most solutions are reactive — therapy and sick leave only come after the damage is done

💡 Our Solution
BurnBrake is an AI-powered burnout predictor that passively analyzes your calendar and work patterns, calculates a real-time burnout risk score, and delivers personalized interventions — before burnout hits.
Think of it as a fitness tracker, but for your mental workload.


✨ Key Features

Burnout Risk Score — A 0–100 gauge updated daily based on your work patterns
Calendar Analysis — Detects back-to-back meetings, skipped breaks, late-night work, and weekend work creep
AI Intervention Plan — Powered by Claude AI, generates a personalized action plan based on your specific patterns
Trend Tracking — Visual graph showing your burnout score over the past 7 days
Activity Heatmap — Monthly calendar view showing high/medium/low risk days
Smart Alerts — Categorized warnings (critical, moderate, informational) based on detected patterns
Quick Interventions — Actionable cards you can check off throughout the day
